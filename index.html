Frequently Asked Questions?

ArtD’Hope Art Community is a social platform for artists to publish, share & discuss art & artworks. It’s a free platform in which you can register using your email address to create your profile.

Art Community & Art Store is curated by a panel of artists to keep the community standards and make everyone align to the community guideline.

You can join the community for free from this link “www.wordpress-487506-2140890.cloudwaysapps.com/register”. By providing your name, email address and password you can start creating your account. You need to verify your email address to activate your account.

The Art Community will send the verification email to your registered email address. If you cant find it in your inbox, please check your SPAM folder, and mark the mail as not SPAM for getting the future mails directly to your inbox. If you still can’t find the verification mail either in your inbox or your spam folder, then please contact the support at hello@artdhope.org.

Artworks published at the art auction house are curated by our team of artists from the community.

  1. Create your account and publish your portfolio & Artwork in the Art Gallery.
  2. Our team of curators will contact you for more details about your Artwork.
  3. Please share more details like artwork dimension and artist price with them to invite you to publish artworks in the Art Auction House.
